A Brief History of Europe was compiled and translated by Joseph E. A. Freeman from the History of Europe by the British scholar E. A. Freeman. Dante Alighieri appears in volume eight of Edkins’ compilation: “The Latin of the Italian region gradually transformed into the modern Italian dialect; this transformation was initiated by a great poet of that land, named Dante Alighieri.”
